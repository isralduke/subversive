jesus said he is

john 8:58

“Jesus said to them, “I tell you the solemn truth, before Abraham came into existence, I am!”
— john 8:58
Read john 8:58 »

Who was Jesus? He is either the devil of hell, a lunatic, or He’s telling the truth.

The startling nature, the stark, bold claim of this statement was offensive enough to the religious leaders of Jesus that they wanted Him dead. They didn’t merely want him ignored or silenced, they conspired to kill him. After He made this statement they tried to stone him, to kill him.

CS Lewis spoke of Jesus’ identity when he talked about whats referred to as Lewis’ trilemma as he implores us not to take Jesus lightly.
