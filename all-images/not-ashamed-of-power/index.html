I am not afraid of power.

In Romans, Paul is talking about the confidence he has in Christ, who can save him, despite his many flaws and sins. While he was ashamed of many things he did before Christ saved him, while he wasn’t sure of even his own religious works, he was very confident in the Gospel’s power to save him.
