consider yourself

philippians 2:4

“Each of you should be concerned not only about your own interests, but about the interests of others as well.”
— philippians 2:4
Read philippians 2:4 »

It’s harder to see other peoples’ needs when our own needs and wants are screaming for our attention.

Our own needs and wants seem much more important. I think we miss the whole key to happiness when we trod over other people in the din our of own self-importance. Personal liberty is abused the moment when we greedily sacrifice others for our own gain (something that many corporations and governments would do well to consider).

Unfortunately, a “me-first” attitude fosters a world wherein others disappear completely as we run amok fulfilling our own desires and wishes, making room for evil to seep into the world around us. If we followed the advice of Philippians 2:4, the world would be a much better place for everyone.
