the beloved son

matthew 3:17

“And lo a voice from heaven, saying, “This is my beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased.””
— matthew 3:17
Read matthew 3:17 »

Every religious checkbox is fulfilled by Jesus.

Scores of churches may tell people that they have to do this or do that, behave like this or like that in order to get to Heaven. But, they’re wrong. And not just wrong, they’re lying.

Jesus has already fulfilled all the requirements of God for us, because we can’t. We can’t be good enough on our own, or good enough for God’s high standards. Belief in Jesus is our God-provided loophole.
