romans 5:8

“But God demonstrates his own love for us, in that while we were still sinners, Christ died for us.”
— romans 5:8
Read romans 5:8 »

You’re not likely to meet a person who volunteers to donate their only shirt off of their own back to you, as well as their jacket before you’ve met them.

Christ did this very act when He chose to demonstrate His love towards the world. Sure, you’re likely to die for someone you think is a good person, right? What if you didn’t think they’re a good person?

romans 5:8 tells us that He chose to do die for us even before we heard about Him! He died for us even though we weren’t good people! In “The World’s Last Night’ CS Lewis wrote that Jesus died for people not because they were good, but to make them good.
