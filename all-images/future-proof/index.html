future proof

jeremiah 29:11

“For I know what I have planned for you,’ says the Lord. ‘I have plans to prosper you, not to harm you. I have plans to give you a future filled with hope.”
— jeremiah 29:11
Read jeremiah 29:11 »

The future is filled with hope if you look to the correct person. God states to the Israelites that He’s going to care for them, to see them through their myriad tribulations, that He’s got good things in store for them!

The top version focuses on the typography. The “I” represents that it is God Himself speaking. It’s the brightest thing in the image because He’s the certainty in the context of our lives and our perception.

There’s two versions for this verse because this is a promise delivered through the prophet Jeremiah to the ancient Israelites.
