Artist Info

Isral Duke is a graphic designer living in Baton Rouge, LA, USA.

Isral Duke has worked as a web designer, an icon designer, ad designer, an illustrator, UI designer, t-shirt designer, and even some product concept design. He has been doing freelance work since incorporating in 2006, diving into self-employment since early 2012. He is a graduate of Louisiana State University in Graphic Design.
